Jerry M. Huang's postdoctoral research focuses on next-generation methods for amortized Bayesian inference with generative AI. A developer of the [BayesFlow](https://bayesflow.org/) framework, he is particularly interested in Bayesian inference and deep learning and their applications in computational models of cognition.

**Education**
- **2020 - 2025** – Ph.D., Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (Virtual Environment and Cognitive Modeling)  
- **2025 - Present** – Postdoctoral Researcher, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (Amortized Bayesian Inference and Cognitive Modeling)
