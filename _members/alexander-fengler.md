Alexander Fengler is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Brown University, in the [Laboratory of Neural Computation and Cognition](https://www.lnccbrown.com). He focuses on probabilistic programming and simulation-based inference (SBI) methods, in particular their application to computational cognitive modeling. Alex has an interest in workflow automation across the Bayesian computational statistics eco-system and is the originator of the [HSSM](https://lnccbrown.github.io/HSSM/) toolbox for Bayesian cognitive modeling with likelihood surrogates.

**Education**
- **2023 – present** – Postdoctoral Research Associate, Brown University
- **2018 – 2023** – Ph.D., Brown University (Cognitive Science)
- **2016 – 2017** – MPhil., Bocconi University (Statistics)
