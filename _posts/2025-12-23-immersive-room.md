---
title: Wandering in the otherworld
author: jerry-huang
---

Imagine being in a room with a group of friends. The room takes you to a virtual tour of a forest projected all around you. On your journey, you encounter objects of nature's wonders--perhaps a giant puffball mushroom, or a singing bluejay nearby. Curiosity guides you, as it does to your peers. Would you choose to explore this otherworld on your own, or would you go with the flow?
